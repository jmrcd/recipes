Beef Salpicao

Description

This savoury Filipino dish features tender cubes of beef (usually rib-eye or sirloin) and a flavourful sauce. Despite the fact that the meat bursts with flavour, it is a fairly quick meal to prepare and cook.

Ingredients

Adjust the ingredients depending on how much beef you plan to cook. The following ingredients are for a serving size of 4 people.

  • 2 lbs of your choice cut of beef, cubed (half an inch or so)
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp butter

Steps

  1. Lightly season your meat with salt and pepper. Mix the soy sauce, oyster sauce, and sugar together in a bowl.
  2. Heat the oil and minced garlic over medium heat. Cook the garlic until golden brown, then remove and set aside.
  3. Increase the heat to medium-high. Add the beef but only cook one layer at a time. Do not stack the cubes over each other, and sear for one minute. Stir fry for an additional 2 minutes at least, then continue until cooked to your liking. Keep in mind that it will go back in again to incorporate the sauce.
  4. Once done with the meat, empty the pan and pour in the sauce, let it boil for a few seconds before putting the beef back in. Add the butter, and keep the heat on low-medium, continue cooking and stirring until melted.
  5. Serve over rice using the garlic as a garnish.
